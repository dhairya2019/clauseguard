SAAS SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

This SaaS Subscription Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of February 15, 2026, by
and between CloudBridge Solutions Pvt. Ltd., a company incorporated under the Companies Act,
2013, with registered office at Bangalore, Karnataka, India ("Vendor"), and Horizon Analytics
Pvt. Ltd., a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, with registered office at
Mumbai, Maharashtra, India ("Customer").

1. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

1.1 Vendor provides a cloud-based analytics and reporting platform ("Service") accessible
via web browser and API, as described in the Service Description (Exhibit A).

1.2 Vendor shall provide reasonable advance notice of material changes to the Service that
may affect Customer's use. Vendor shall maintain backward compatibility for documented APIs
for a minimum of twelve (12) months after announcing deprecation.

2. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

2.1 Vendor commits to a monthly Service availability of 99.9% ("Uptime SLA"), measured on
a calendar month basis. Scheduled maintenance windows shall be excluded from uptime
calculations and shall be communicated at least 48 hours in advance.

2.2 If Service availability falls below the Uptime SLA in any calendar month, Customer
shall receive service credits as follows:
    (a) 99.0% to 99.9%: 10% credit on monthly fees
    (b) 95.0% to 99.0%: 25% credit on monthly fees
    (c) Below 95.0%: 50% credit on monthly fees

2.3 Vendor shall provide Customer access to a real-time status dashboard showing current
and historical Service availability.

3. SUBSCRIPTION TERM AND RENEWAL

3.1 The initial subscription term is twelve (12) months from the date of this Agreement.

3.2 The subscription shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods
unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days prior
to the end of the then-current term.

3.3 Annual subscription pricing shall remain locked at the rate agreed upon for the Initial
Term. Any pricing changes for Renewal Terms shall be communicated at least sixty (60) days
in advance and shall not exceed 8% of the prior term's fees without mutual written agreement.

4. FEES AND PAYMENT

4.1 Customer shall pay the annual subscription fee of INR 12,00,000 in advance within
thirty (30) days of invoice.

4.2 Late payments shall accrue interest at 1% per month. Vendor may suspend access after
thirty (30) days of non-payment, following written notice.

5. DATA OWNERSHIP AND PORTABILITY

5.1 Customer retains full ownership of all data uploaded to or generated within the Service
("Customer Data"). Vendor acquires no rights in Customer Data except as necessary to provide
the Service.

5.2 Vendor provides a Data Export API that allows Customer to export all Customer Data in
standard formats (CSV, JSON, or Parquet) at any time during the subscription.

5.3 Upon termination or expiration, Vendor shall make Customer Data available for export for
a period of sixty (60) days. After this period, Vendor shall securely delete Customer Data
and provide written certification of deletion within ninety (90) days.

6. SECURITY AND COMPLIANCE

6.1 Vendor shall maintain commercially reasonable security measures to protect Customer Data,
including encryption at rest and in transit, access controls, and regular security assessments.

6.2 Vendor shall notify Customer of any data breach affecting Customer Data within seventy-two
(72) hours of becoming aware of such breach.

6.3 Vendor shall comply with applicable data protection laws, including the Digital Personal
Data Protection Act, 2023.

7. INDEMNIFICATION

7.1 Each party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other from third-party claims arising
from the indemnifying party's breach of this Agreement, negligence, or willful misconduct.

7.2 Vendor shall indemnify Customer from third-party claims that the Service infringes any
intellectual property right.

8. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

8.1 Each party's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total
fees paid or payable by Customer during the twelve (12) months preceding the event giving
rise to the claim.

8.2 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, special, or
punitive damages, except in cases of gross negligence, willful misconduct, or breach of
confidentiality obligations.

9. TERMINATION

9.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach if the breaching party
fails to cure within thirty (30) days of written notice specifying the breach.

9.2 Customer may terminate for convenience at the end of any subscription term by providing
notice as specified in Section 3.2.

9.3 Upon termination, the data export provisions of Section 5.3 shall apply.

10. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

10.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

10.2 Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be resolved by
arbitration administered by the Mumbai Centre for International Arbitration (MCIA). The seat
of arbitration shall be Mumbai, India.

11. GENERAL PROVISIONS

11.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. Amendments require
the written agreement of both parties.

11.2 Neither party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other
party, except in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets.
