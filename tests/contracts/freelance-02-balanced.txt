INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

This Independent Contractor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of February 1, 2026,
by and between Pinnacle Solutions Pvt. Ltd., a company incorporated under the Companies Act,
2013, having its registered office at Mumbai, Maharashtra, India ("Client"), and Priya Sharma,
an independent contractor residing in Pune, Maharashtra, India ("Contractor").

1. SCOPE OF WORK

Contractor shall provide UI/UX design services as described in Exhibit A attached hereto.
Deliverables, milestones, specifications, and acceptance criteria are defined in Exhibit A.
Any changes to the scope of work shall require a written change order signed by both parties,
specifying the revised deliverables, adjusted timeline, and additional fees if applicable.

2. COMPENSATION AND PAYMENT

2.1 Total project fee: INR 8,00,000 (Rupees Eight Lakhs).

2.2 Payment shall be made in three milestone installments:
    (a) INR 2,50,000 upon execution of this Agreement;
    (b) INR 3,00,000 upon completion and acceptance of Milestone 2 (as defined in Exhibit A);
    (c) INR 2,50,000 upon completion and acceptance of final deliverables.

2.3 Each milestone payment is due within 30 days of invoice (Net 30). Invoices not paid
within 30 days shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law,
whichever is lower.

2.4 Acceptance of deliverables shall be based on conformance to the specifications in
Exhibit A. Client shall provide written acceptance or specific written objections within
10 business days of delivery. Failure to respond within 10 business days shall constitute
acceptance.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

3.1 Contractor assigns to Client all intellectual property rights in deliverables created
specifically for this project, as defined in Exhibit A.

3.2 Contractor retains ownership of all pre-existing intellectual property, tools, frameworks,
and methodologies ("Pre-Existing IP"). To the extent Pre-Existing IP is incorporated into
deliverables, Contractor grants Client a non-exclusive, perpetual, royalty-free license to use
such Pre-Existing IP solely as part of the deliverables.

3.3 Contractor is granted a non-exclusive license to use deliverables in portfolio and case
studies with client-identifying details removed, after a 6-month embargo period from project
completion.

4. NON-COMPETE

During the term of this Agreement, Contractor shall not perform substantially similar UI/UX
design services for the following named competitors of Client: DesignHub India Pvt. Ltd. and
CreativeForce Technologies Pvt. Ltd. This restriction terminates upon expiration or termination
of this Agreement. For clarity, Contractor's confidentiality obligations under Section 6 survive
termination, but no restriction on Contractor's right to compete or work in their field of
expertise shall apply after termination.

5. TERMINATION

5.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement with 14 days' written notice for any reason.

5.2 Either party may terminate immediately upon material breach if the breaching party fails
to cure within 14 days of written notice specifying the breach.

5.3 Upon termination for any reason, Client shall pay for all work completed and accepted
through the termination date, plus a pro-rata share of any in-progress milestone based on
percentage of completion as mutually agreed.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 Each party shall maintain the confidentiality of information designated as confidential
by the disclosing party for a period of two (2) years following termination.

6.2 Confidential Information expressly excludes: (a) information publicly available through
no fault of the receiving party; (b) information independently developed without use of the
disclosing party's information; (c) information received from a third party without restriction;
(d) information required to be disclosed by law or court order, with prompt notice to the
disclosing party.

7. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

7.1 Each party's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total
fees paid or payable under this Agreement.

7.2 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, special, or
punitive damages.

7.3 Each party shall indemnify and hold harmless the other from third-party claims arising
from the indemnifying party's breach of this Agreement or negligence.

8. GOVERNING LAW AND DISPUTE RESOLUTION

8.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

8.2 Any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved by arbitration
administered by the Mumbai Centre for International Arbitration (MCIA) in accordance with its
rules. The seat of arbitration shall be Mumbai, India. The language of arbitration shall be
English.

9. GENERAL PROVISIONS

9.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. Amendments require
written agreement signed by both parties.

9.2 Neither party may assign this Agreement without the written consent of the other party.
