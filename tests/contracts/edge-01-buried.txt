PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Professional Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of March 5, 2026,
by and between Apex Digital Corp., a corporation organized under the laws of the State of
New York, United States ("Client"), and Deepika Iyer, an independent contractor residing
in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India ("Contractor").

1. DEFINITIONS

1.1 "Engagement" means the exclusive, full-time provision of services by Contractor to
Client, during which Contractor shall not provide any services of any nature to any other
person or entity, whether for compensation or otherwise, without Client's prior written
consent. The Engagement shall commence on the date of this Agreement and continue until
terminated in accordance with Section 5.

1.2 "Deliverables" means all work product, outputs, and materials created by Contractor
in connection with the Engagement, as further described in Exhibit A.

1.3 "Standard Terms" means the terms and conditions set forth in Sections 1 through 10
of this Agreement, which have been adopted as the standard terms for all of Client's
professional services engagements.

2. SCOPE OF WORK

Contractor shall provide web application development services as described in Exhibit A.
Contractor shall perform services in accordance with Client's quality standards and
specifications. Acceptance of deliverables shall be at Client's reasonable satisfaction.

3. COMPENSATION

3.1 Client shall pay Contractor a monthly retainer of USD 8,000 during the Engagement.

3.2 Payments shall be made within forty-five (45) days of invoice. Each invoice must be
submitted within five (5) business days following the end of the applicable month.

3.3 In the event of a dispute regarding any invoice, Client may withhold payment of the
disputed amount pending resolution.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY

4.1 Contractor acknowledges that during the Engagement, Contractor will have access to
Client's confidential and proprietary information.

4.2 Contractor shall maintain the confidentiality of all information disclosed by Client
for a period of three (3) years following termination of this Agreement.

4.3 All materials, documents, software, code, designs, architectures, and derivative works
created by Contractor during the Engagement, whether or not related to Client's business
and whether or not created during working hours, shall be the sole property of Client.
Contractor hereby irrevocably assigns all right, title, and interest in such materials to
Client, including all intellectual property rights, patent rights, copyright, trade secret
rights, and any other proprietary rights. Contractor waives all moral rights and rights of
attribution in such materials.

4.4 Contractor shall not incorporate any pre-existing intellectual property or third-party
materials into the Deliverables without Client's prior written approval.

5. TERM AND TERMINATION

5.1 This Agreement shall commence on the date first written above and shall continue for
an initial term of twelve (12) months.

5.2 Client may terminate this Agreement at any time with seven (7) days written notice.
Contractor may terminate this Agreement with sixty (60) days written notice.

5.3 Upon termination, Client shall pay Contractor for services rendered through the
termination date, less any amounts subject to dispute.

6. MUTUAL OBLIGATIONS

6.1 Contractor agrees to the following obligations, which shall survive termination:

    (a) Contractor shall not disclose any of Client's confidential information to any
        third party;
    (b) Contractor shall not use any of Client's confidential information for personal
        benefit or for the benefit of any third party;
    (c) Contractor shall not, for a period of eighteen (18) months following termination,
        provide services to any entity that is, or is reasonably likely to become, a
        competitor of Client in any geographic market;
    (d) Contractor shall not, for a period of eighteen (18) months following termination,
        solicit or recruit any employee, contractor, or consultant of Client;
    (e) Contractor shall not, for a period of eighteen (18) months following termination,
        solicit any customer or client of Client with whom Contractor had any contact
        during the Engagement;
    (f) All intellectual property created during the Engagement remains Client's property
        in perpetuity.

6.2 Client agrees to the following obligations:

    (a) Client shall provide Contractor with reasonable access to information and resources
        necessary for the performance of services;
    (b) Client shall designate a point of contact for communication.

7. STANDARD TERMS

7.1 Contractor shall indemnify and hold harmless Client from any claims, damages, or losses
arising out of Contractor's performance under this Agreement. There shall be no cap on
Contractor's indemnification obligation.

7.2 Client's liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the fees paid to Contractor
in the one (1) month preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

7.3 Any dispute shall be resolved exclusively in the state and federal courts of New York,
New York, under New York law. Contractor waives any objection to personal jurisdiction.

8. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

Contractor represents and warrants that: (a) Contractor has the right and authority to enter
into this Agreement; (b) the Deliverables will not infringe any third-party intellectual
property rights; (c) Contractor will perform services in a professional and workmanlike
manner.

9. FORCE MAJEURE

Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by events beyond its reasonable control,
including natural disasters, war, pandemic, or government action.

10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. This Agreement may be
amended only by a written instrument signed by both parties.
