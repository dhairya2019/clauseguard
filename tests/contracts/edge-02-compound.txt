CONSULTING SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Consulting Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of February 20, 2026,
by and between FutureScale Inc., a corporation organized under the laws of the State of
Texas, United States ("Client"), and Karthik Venkataraman, an independent consultant
residing in Bangalore, Karnataka, India ("Consultant").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Client desires to engage Consultant to provide data engineering consulting services;
and

WHEREAS, Consultant possesses the skills and expertise required to perform such services;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree:

1. SCOPE OF WORK

1.1 Consultant shall provide data engineering consulting services as described in Exhibit B,
which is incorporated by reference into this Agreement and sets forth the detailed scope,
deliverables, timeline, and acceptance criteria.

1.2 Consultant acknowledges that additional tasks and responsibilities may be assigned in
accordance with Company Policy, which may be updated from time to time at Client's discretion.

2. COMPENSATION

2.1 Client shall pay Consultant a project fee of USD 50,000, payable upon completion of all
deliverables and acceptance of the final work product at Client's sole discretion.

2.2 Payment shall be made within sixty (60) days of Client's written acceptance. For clarity,
payment shall be due upon completion AND acceptance at Client's sole discretion, and no
partial payments or milestone payments shall apply.

2.3 Consultant's fee includes all expenses, taxes, and costs associated with the performance
of services. No additional reimbursement shall be provided.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY AND NON-COMPETE

3.1 Consultant hereby assigns to Client all intellectual property rights in all work product
AND agrees not to compete with Client or any of its affiliates for a period of twenty-four
(24) months following termination of this Agreement, anywhere in the world.

3.2 Work product includes all materials, tools, ideas, concepts, methodologies, algorithms,
data models, and documentation created, developed, conceived, or contributed to by Consultant
in connection with this Agreement, whether or not incorporated into the final deliverables.

3.3 Consultant waives all moral rights, including rights of attribution and integrity, in
all work product.

3.4 The intellectual property assignment and non-compete obligations in this Section 3 shall
survive termination of this Agreement.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY AND RESTRICTIONS

4.1 Consultant shall maintain strict confidentiality of all Client information, including
business strategies, customer data, technical architecture, and financial information, in
perpetuity.

4.2 Consultant shall not disclose any information to any person or entity, including in
response to legal process, without Client's prior written consent.

4.3 Consultant shall not, during the term of this Agreement or for eighteen (18) months
thereafter, solicit or recruit any employee, contractor, or consultant of Client or any of
its affiliates, or solicit any customer, vendor, or business partner of Client.

5. TERMINATION

5.1 Client may terminate this Agreement at any time for any reason with five (5) days
written notice. Upon termination by Client, Consultant shall be paid for work completed
and accepted prior to the termination date, as determined by Client in its sole discretion.

5.2 Consultant may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days written notice. Upon
termination by Consultant, all work product created through the termination date shall be
delivered to Client and all intellectual property therein shall be deemed assigned to Client.

5.3 Sections 3, 4, and 6 shall survive termination of this Agreement.

6. INDEMNIFICATION AND LIABILITY

6.1 Consultant shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Client from any and all claims,
including but not limited to intellectual property infringement claims, regardless of whether
such claims arise from Client's modification, combination, or use of the work product in
ways not contemplated by Consultant.

6.2 Consultant's total liability under this Agreement shall be unlimited.

6.3 Client's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the lesser of: (a) the
fees actually paid to Consultant, or (b) USD 10,000.

7. REPRESENTATIONS

7.1 Consultant represents that Consultant is an independent contractor and not an employee
of Client. Consultant is responsible for all taxes and statutory obligations.

7.2 Consultant represents that the deliverables shall conform to the specifications in
Exhibit B and shall not infringe any third-party rights. Note: Exhibit B is to be provided
separately by Client and is not attached to this Agreement at the time of execution.

8. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Texas, United States. Any
dispute shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Austin, Texas.

9. GENERAL PROVISIONS

9.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. Amendments require
written agreement signed by both parties.

9.2 Client may assign this Agreement to any entity without Consultant's consent. Consultant
may not assign this Agreement without Client's prior written consent.

9.3 Terms defined in Company Policy (as amended from time to time) are incorporated into
this Agreement by reference.
