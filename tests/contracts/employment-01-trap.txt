EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 10, 2026, by and
between NexGen Innovations Pvt. Ltd., a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013,
with registered office at Gurugram, Haryana, India ("Employer"), and Arjun Mehta, residing
in New Delhi, India ("Employee").

1. POSITION AND DUTIES

1.1 Employee is appointed as Senior Software Engineer and shall report to the Engineering
Director. Employee shall perform such duties as assigned by the Employer from time to time,
including duties beyond the scope of the primary role.

1.2 Employee shall devote full working time, attention, and energy to the business of the
Employer. Employee shall not engage in any outside employment, business activity, consulting,
or freelance work without the prior written consent of the Employer.

2. COMPENSATION

2.1 Employee shall receive an annual gross salary of INR 24,00,000 (Rupees Twenty-Four Lakhs),
payable monthly.

2.2 Salary revisions shall be at the sole discretion of the Employer. The Employer is under
no obligation to provide annual increments or bonuses.

3. EMPLOYMENT BOND

3.1 In consideration of the training, resources, and proprietary knowledge provided by the
Employer, Employee agrees to remain in the employment of the Employer for a minimum period of
twenty-four (24) months from the date of joining ("Bond Period").

3.2 If Employee voluntarily resigns or is terminated for cause during the Bond Period, Employee
shall pay the Employer liquidated damages equal to twenty-four (24) months of gross salary
(INR 48,00,000) as compensation for the Employer's investment in training and development.

3.3 The Employer may deduct the bond amount from any final settlement dues payable to the
Employee. If final settlement dues are insufficient, the Employee shall pay the remaining
balance within thirty (30) days of separation.

4. NOTICE PERIOD

4.1 Employee must provide ninety (90) days written notice prior to resignation. During the
notice period, Employee shall continue to perform all duties and responsibilities.

4.2 Employer may terminate Employee's employment at any time by providing one (1) day written
notice, or payment of one (1) day salary in lieu of notice.

4.3 Employer may terminate Employee's employment immediately without notice for cause,
including but not limited to: failure to meet performance expectations as determined by
Employer in its sole discretion, insubordination, unauthorized disclosure, or any other
reason deemed appropriate by the Employer.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1 All intellectual property created, conceived, developed, or reduced to practice by
Employee during the term of employment, whether during working hours or personal time,
whether using Employer resources or not, and whether or not related to the Employer's
business, shall be the sole and exclusive property of the Employer.

5.2 This includes but is not limited to: inventions, discoveries, improvements, designs,
software, algorithms, methodologies, trade secrets, and all other forms of intellectual
property, including personal side projects, open source contributions, blog posts, and
educational materials created during the employment period.

5.3 Employee hereby irrevocably assigns all right, title, and interest in such intellectual
property to the Employer.

6. NON-COMPETE

6.1 Employee agrees that for a period of twenty-four (24) months following the termination
or expiration of employment, regardless of the reason for separation, Employee shall not
directly or indirectly:

    (a) Engage in any business that competes with the Employer's business;
    (b) Provide services (whether as an employee, consultant, contractor, or otherwise) to
        any entity that competes with the Employer;
    (c) Solicit any customer, client, vendor, or business partner of the Employer;
    (d) Recruit or hire any employee of the Employer.

6.2 This restriction applies worldwide, without geographic limitation.

6.3 No garden leave payment or compensation shall be provided during the non-compete period.

7. PROBATION

Employee shall be on probation for a period of six (6) months from the date of joining.
During probation, Employer may terminate employment with one (1) day notice. Confirmation
of employment after probation is at the Employer's sole discretion.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY

Employee shall not disclose any confidential information of the Employer, including business
strategies, client information, technical data, and financial information, during or after
employment. This obligation continues in perpetuity.

9. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. Any
dispute shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Gurugram, Haryana.

10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior
agreements, understandings, and negotiations. This Agreement may be amended only by a written
instrument signed by the Employer.
