INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR AGREEMENT

This Independent Contractor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2026,
by and between TechCorp Inc., a corporation organized under the laws of the State of Delaware,
United States ("Client"), and Rajesh Kumar, an individual residing in Bangalore, Karnataka,
India ("Contractor").

1. SCOPE OF WORK

Contractor shall provide software development services as directed by Client from time to time.
Contractor shall perform such other duties as may be reasonably assigned by Client. Deliverables
shall be deemed acceptable at Client's sole discretion.

2. COMPENSATION

Client shall pay Contractor a fixed fee of USD 15,000 for the complete project. Payment shall
be made upon completion of all deliverables and final acceptance at Client's sole discretion.
Payment terms are Net 90 from the date of Client's written acceptance. No milestone payments
or progress billing shall apply. Client reserves the right to offset any amounts owed against
claims for defective work.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

All work product, including but not limited to all intellectual property, ideas, concepts,
methodologies, tools, frameworks, and pre-existing IP created, developed, or used by Contractor
in connection with this Agreement, shall be the sole and exclusive property of Client. Contractor
hereby assigns all right, title, and interest in and to all work product to Client. Contractor
waives all moral rights in the work product, including rights of attribution and integrity.
This IP assignment shall survive termination of this Agreement.

4. NON-COMPETE

Contractor agrees that for a period of twenty-four (24) months following the termination or
expiration of this Agreement, Contractor shall not, directly or indirectly, anywhere in the
world, engage in, own, manage, operate, consult for, or provide services to any business that
competes with Client's business or any of its affiliates. This restriction applies regardless
of the reason for termination.

5. TERMINATION

Client may terminate this Agreement at any time, for any reason or no reason, with immediate
effect upon written notice to Contractor. Upon termination by Client, no payment shall be due
for work completed or in progress. Contractor may not terminate this Agreement without Client's
prior written consent.

6. INDEMNIFICATION

Contractor shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Client and its officers, directors,
employees, agents, successors, and assigns from and against any and all claims, damages, losses,
costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorney's fees) arising out of or relating to
Contractor's performance under this Agreement, regardless of the cause or origin of such claims.
There shall be no limitation on the amount of Contractor's indemnification obligation.

7. LIABILITY

Client shall not be liable to Contractor for any indirect, incidental, consequential, special,
or punitive damages. Client's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the amount
of fees actually paid to Contractor. Contractor's liability to Client shall be unlimited.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY

Contractor acknowledges that all information provided by Client is confidential in perpetuity.
Contractor shall not disclose any information to any third party without Client's prior written
consent, including information required to be disclosed by law or court order.

9. GOVERNING LAW AND JURISDICTION

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of
Delaware, United States. Any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be
subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the state and federal courts located in Wilmington,
Delaware. Contractor waives any right to bring proceedings in any other jurisdiction.

10. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior
negotiations, representations, warranties, commitments, offers, and agreements, whether written
or oral.
