SAAS SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

This SaaS Subscription Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 20, 2026, by
and between DataVault Inc., a corporation organized under the laws of the State of California,
United States ("Vendor"), and Meridian Enterprises Pvt. Ltd., a company incorporated under
the Companies Act, 2013, with registered office at Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India ("Customer").

1. SERVICE DESCRIPTION

1.1 Vendor provides a cloud-based enterprise data management platform ("Service") accessible
via web browser and API.

1.2 Vendor reserves the right to modify, update, or discontinue any feature or functionality
of the Service at any time, in its sole discretion, without prior notice to Customer. Vendor
shall have no liability for any modification, suspension, or discontinuation of the Service.

2. SUBSCRIPTION TERM AND RENEWAL

2.1 The initial subscription term is twelve (12) months from the date of this Agreement
("Initial Term").

2.2 The subscription shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods
("Renewal Terms") unless Customer provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90)
days prior to the end of the then-current term.

2.3 Vendor may adjust subscription pricing for any Renewal Term at Vendor's discretion.
Pricing may change at Vendor's sole discretion and there shall be no cap or limitation on
price increases. Customer's continued use of the Service after a price change constitutes
acceptance of the new pricing.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT

3.1 Customer shall pay the annual subscription fee of USD 120,000 in advance within thirty
(30) days of invoice.

3.2 All fees are non-refundable. No credits shall be issued for partial months, downtime,
or unused services.

3.3 Late payments shall accrue interest at the rate of 1.5% per month. Vendor may suspend
access to the Service for any payment more than fifteen (15) days overdue.

4. DATA AND CONTENT

4.1 Customer retains ownership of data uploaded to the Service ("Customer Data"). However,
Customer grants Vendor a worldwide, perpetual, irrevocable license to use, analyze, and
aggregate Customer Data for Vendor's internal purposes, including product improvement,
analytics, and benchmarking.

4.2 Vendor shall have no obligation to provide data portability, data export, or data
migration functionality. Customer acknowledges that its data may be stored in proprietary
formats and that export in a standard format is not guaranteed.

4.3 Upon termination, Vendor may delete all Customer Data within thirty (30) days. Vendor
shall have no obligation to maintain or provide Customer Data after termination.

Vendor does not guarantee any specific level of service availability, performance, or
uptime. The Service is provided "AS IS" and "AS AVAILABLE." Vendor makes no representations

8.1 Vendor may terminate this Agreement at any time for any reason by providing thirty (30)
